In today's digital landscape, cloud computing has revolutionized the way businesses operate, providing a huge advantage at scalability, flexibility, and cost efficiency when deploying a digital system. One of the big players of cloud computing is Amazon Web Services (AWS), a leading cloud services provider that offers a wide range of tools and services to help organizations build robust and scalable applications.

Navigating the world of AWS can be overwhelming due the extensive documentations and multiple application approaches, what you need as a beginner is understanding fundamental concepts of AWS architecture. That's where this blog comes into play. This blog will describe AWS architecture fundamentals and provide you with basic examples of its application so you can dive into deeper and more complex examples by your own.
